Better call Sara

My interest in brand communication led me to study Advertising & PR where I discovered that I was not only interested in design, I was also fascinated by the part of planner, analytics and consumer behavior. So, after improving the graphic part studying a master's degree in graphic design, I decided to give a more technological nuance to these issues and enter the world of UX UI. I found again this interest in the consumer and how he makes buying decisions and so, while I continue working in UX UI, I try to better understand this process in the consumer's mind in order to improve user experiences by studying a master's degree in neuromarketing.
